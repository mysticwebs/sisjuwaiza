ADMISSION PROCEDURE FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2023-24



The parents of prospective students need to complete the online application form as per the guidelines. Additional information about the admission procedures and other details regarding the date of submission of printed registered form, fee details etc., will be given on the school website later

Note:
1. Name, DOB etc. should be as per EID/Passport of the student.
2. Name of the parents should be as given in the child’s Passport
3. Parents are requested to read and follow the instructions given. (TC and Mark list Attestation, deadlines etc.)
4. Forms with wrong / incomplete entries will be rejected.
5. Registration Fee of AED 200 should be paid (at the cash counter either IAS/SIS) after the completion of Online Registration Form.
6. Online Registration Doesn’t guarantee admissions and Registration Fee is Non refundable.
7.Further steps, Details of Entry Level Tests etc, will be communicated through website only. Hence you are required to visit the website for further updates.
8.Please note that no further communications will be sent regarding admission to you by sms or mail. All information will be provided through website only.
You are therefore required to look up the school website from time to time

Age Criteria

Grade-I
6 years as on 31st March 2023



Steps to be followed for taking the Print out


Step 1: Go to the online registration form.
Step 2: Scroll down, you will see the ‘View’ button.
Step 3: Click on the ‘View’ button.
Step 4: Enter your Registration number and the Date of Birth of the child and click the ‘Get Details’ button, then you will be able to view your application.
Step 5: Click on the ‘Print’ button.
